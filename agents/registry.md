## Agent ID Guidelines

Use stable IDs that identify the person/device/session role, for example:

- `alice-pc-coder`
- `bob-pc-designer`
- `alice-mobile-reviewer`

## Notes

Agent IDs are for coordination and handoffs. They do not need to match any specific AI tool or account name.
